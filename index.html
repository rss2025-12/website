About Us

In the spring of 2025, a bunch of chill guys and girls enrolled in 6.4200: Robotics Science and Systems.

We all found ourselves in Team 12, tasked with programming a robot car in ROS2 Humble. We were assigned labs, which required the car to perform various tasks autonomously. We grinded through every lab, learning about robotics, communication, and teamwork along the way.

Inspired by the instructions "Hold left bumper (RB)", we named our car RuBy.
